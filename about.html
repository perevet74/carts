About Lost Mary

Designing The Future of Flavour

Lost Mary was founded to push beyond traditional forms, blending bold aesthetics, progressive technology and responsible flavour experiences. We prototype relentlessly and partner with global flavour scientists to keep the Lost Mary experience unmistakable.

Discover QUAQ Tech Meet the Team

Our Design DNA

From gradient finishes to soft-touch housings and light-responsive halos, every Lost Mary device is crafted to feel as expressive as it looks. We take cues from street culture, fashion capsules and experiential art to keep each release distinctive.

Our industrial designers obsess over ergonomics and materials, iterating dozens of prototypes to ensure every edge, angle and grip point supports all-day flavour sessions.

Flavour Fidelity

Lost Mary collaborates with leading flavour laboratories to map sensory feedback, heat curves and vapour density. QUAQ mesh 2.0 ensures the first puff mirrors the last without flavour fade or harshness.

  • Global flavour lab partnerships
  • 24/7 batch monitoring
  • Sensory mapping for each release

Responsible Innovation

Sustainability informs every decision—from FSC-certified packaging to end-of-life collection programmes and regional partnerships that recycle device components.

We continually evaluate materials and manufacturing processes to reduce waste and environmental impact without compromising experience.
