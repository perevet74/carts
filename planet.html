Planet Wonderland Experiences

Our travelling installations showcase recyclable materials, interactive art, and educational pop-ups that bring the Lost Mary universe to life.

Walk through flavour-inspired landscapes featuring recycled textiles, kinetic lighting, and augmented visuals. Explore prototypes made with bio-based polymers, aluminium, and recycled plastics sourced through partner programs.

Community Workshops

Local artists and sustainability leaders co-host sessions on repurposing devices and creative upcycling. These immersive zones blend education with hands-on creativity.

Material labs showcase our commitment to circular design, demonstrating how Lost Mary devices can be transformed into art pieces and functional objects.

Sustainable Design Actions

  • Transitioning to FSC-certified paper and soy-based inks across all packaging.
  • Implementing regional take-back programs to responsibly recycle disposed devices.
  • Partnering with artists to upcycle components into limited edition art pieces.
  • Funding environmental non-profits through the Lost Mary Planet grant program.

Creative Collaborations

Lost Mary Planet bridges technology and art, working with emerging creators to transform device components into sculptures, installations, and functional design objects.

Our grant program supports artists who explore themes of sustainability, circularity, and responsible consumption through their work.
